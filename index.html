Frequently asked questions

EIYARO is an innovative blockchain project that aims to provide global users with secure and reliable digital asset trading services through efficient and low-cost blockchain technology.

The EIYARO project uses a public chain architecture, which means that anyone can participate in the maintenance and development of the network without having to obtain specific permissions or authorisations. The public chain guarantees a high degree of data transparency and decentralised nature, ensuring the security and neutrality of the network. All participants have the right to view the transaction records on the chain and work together to maintain the normal operation of the network by mining or verifying transactions.

The total amount of coins is 2.1 billion EY, with an initial block reward of 1,000 EY, and a decreasing reward of 10% for every 175,200 blocks. The founding team received 210 million EY from the Genesis Block Rewards, which will be used for community building, developer and contributor rewards, in addition to rewarding several co-founders.

You can get EY by using CPU and GPU mining in Pool.

The EIYARO project will provide EY holders and investors with multiple wallet options: Node wallet, PC wallet, mobile wallet. As an open source project, users are free to contact third-party wallets to request EY integration.

Our Vision

We are committed to building an open and inclusive community, encouraging developers, enterprises and individuals around the world to participate in the construction of the EIYARO ecosystem, and jointly promoting the development and application of blockchain technology. In this process, we will always adhere to the principles of user first and security first, and work hard to build a better future digital world.

Creating communities

The EIYARO project adheres to the principle of community self-governance, and we believe that the strength of the community is the core driving force behind the development of the project. An open community discussion platform has been established to encourage members to make suggestions for improvement and to decide on major matters through a voting system.

The core team of EIYARO project consists of a group of freelancers who love blockchain technology and come from different countries and fields, including computer science, cryptography, distributed systems, finance and so on. Their common goal is to promote the development of blockchain technology and create value for the community and users through the EIYARO project.

In order to better promote the development of the project, the EIYARO project will invite a number of experts and scholars in the blockchain field to serve as advisors. These advisors have rich experience and deep insights in blockchain technology, business models, laws and regulations, etc., providing valuable advice and guidance for the project's strategic planning and development.
